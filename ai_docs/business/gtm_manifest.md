# 4. Solution

## How MerchantDex Works

**CSV Import:** 

Upload trade history CSVs from Binance, OKX, Bybit, KuCoin, MEXC. System auto-parses different formats and normalizes data.

**Profit Calculation:** 

System calculates actual profit per trade based on your buy/sell prices.

**Private Trade Entry:** 

Quick-add form for off-exchange deals ‚Äî client name, amount, price, payment method, notes. Takes 10 seconds per trade.

**Client Database:**

Auto-groups trades by client. See lifetime volume, average margin, last trade date for each customer.

**Unified Dashboard:** 

See all trades in one view. Filter by exchange, client, date range, payment method.

**Export Reports:**

Generate monthly P&L, tax-ready transaction logs, client summaries.

## Key Features

‚ñ∏ **Smart CSV Parser** ‚Äî 

Drag-drop CSVs from any supported exchange. Auto-detects format, maps columns, handles duplicates.

**Profit Analytics** ‚Äî 

Dashboard showing total volume, total profit, average margin %. Breakdown by exchange vs private, payment method, client tier, time period. Trend charts for weekly/monthly profit trajectory.

**Private Trade Logger** ‚Äî 

Quick entry for client, amount, buy/sell price, payment method, date. Link to Telegram username, add notes, attach payment proof. Recurring clients auto-fill from previous trades.

**Report Generator** ‚Äî Monthly P&L summary (PDF/CSV), tax export with all transactions, client-specific statements for dispute resolution.

**Client CRM** ‚Äî 

Each client gets a profile with full trade history. Tags (VIP, new, risky). Lifetime stats: total volume, average margin, trade frequency. Notes field for context.

**Mobile-Friendly** ‚Äî

**Discovery channels:** Telegram groups (P2P trading communities 30K-100K+ members), YouTube (P2P arbitrage tutorials), Twitter/X (crypto trading threads), Exchange forums (Binance Square, OKX community)

- Reward: 1 month free for referrer + 50% off first month for referee
